## Project Overview

Royal Keys Barber Shop is a local barber business that provides modern and classic haircuts, grooming, and styling services. The purpose of this website is to create an online presence for the business, allowing customers to view services, explore hairstyle options, and easily contact the barber shop.

The website is designed to be simple, user-friendly, and visually appealing, ensuring that users can quickly find the information they need.

## Website Goals and Objectives

- To promote Royal Keys Barber Shop and its services
- To attract new customers
- To provide important information such as services, pricing, and contact details
- To showcase different hairstyles through a gallery

Key objectives include:
- Increasing website visitors
- Improving customer engagement
- Making it easier for customers to contact or visit the shop
